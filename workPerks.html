GREAT WORK PERKS


As a PIASC employee, you get discounts to Disneyland, Restaurants, Movie Theaters, Spas, Sporting Events, & So Much More!


Once a month GWP will send out an email highlighting different company perks you are entitled to. You should also make sure to check their website on a weekly basis because we will be constantly adding fun things for you and your family.

Instructions To Check Out Your Perks

  • Visit this page to find out what perks you are entitled to.
  • New perks are added every week, so make sure to check on a regular basis.
  • If you sign up for it, they will also send out an email on a regular basis alerting you to the new perks.

Other Important Info

  • To get a membership card click here
  • All perks will have redemption instructions. Some will require you to show your card, and others can be redeemed directly online.
  • To be added to a weekly e-newsletter to automatically be notified about the latest perks click here

Sample Of Your Perks

  • Burke Williams Up To 25% Off (Must pre-purchase using link on our site)
  • Up to 50% off restaurants
  • Disneyland and other Amusement Parks like SeaWorld, LegoLand, Six Flags and more to $20 off per ticket
  • Movie & Theatrical ticket discounts
  • Fitness center discounts, like Yogaworks, Bootcamps and more
  • LA Clippers, Dodgers & Galaxy up to 50% off tickets

We hope you enjoy the perks!
